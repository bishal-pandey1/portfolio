Who I Am

About Me

I’m Bishal Pandey — a passionate civil sub-engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal, currently studying Civil Engineering. I have practical experience in structural design, building layout, quantity estimation, and construction supervision, and I am committed to delivering safe, efficient, and cost-effective engineering solutions.

I combine academic learning with real-world site experience, working on projects ranging from residential homes to community infrastructure. I am building my expertise in modern engineering software and earthquake-resistant design as I work toward completing my degree.

When I’m not studying or on site, you’ll find me exploring the latest advancements in civil engineering or trekking through the beautiful landscapes of Nepal.
